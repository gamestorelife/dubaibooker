Location: 1st Level, Fairmont Dubai, Sheikh Zayed Road, Trade Centre Area - Dubai
Opens: Every evening from 7 PM to 3 AM

Your Traditional Greek Dining Experience in Dubai

A single night, a lifetime of stories. At OPA, plates are shared, smashed, and savoured while the music pulls you to your feet.

Within The Fairmont Dubai, OPA feels like a postcard from a distant village – bougainvillaea overhead, the scent of jasmine (and olives), and stories shared across every table.

It all began on a small island in Greece. In a quaint, charming kitchen, a gentleman named Kyriakos spent years perfecting his favorite Greek dishes, sharing the rich and delicious flavors with his friends and family.
With his appetite for tender meats and zesty salads, the Zorba dance, and smashing plates, Kyriakos excitedly began to build his traditional restaurant in Dubai.
